OVERVIEW

One motif, every format.

At Sportstech I produced the brand’s display advertising — taking one hero campaign motif per product and rebuilding it across the full set of IAB standard banner sizes used across our media channels.

Every size keeps the same promise — “Dein Training beginnt … direkt zu Hause!” — with logo, headline and call-to-action recomposed for each aspect ratio, so the ad stays legible and on-brand from a slim skyscraper to a wide billboard.
